# 📈 International Education Center: 10-Year Operational Strategy & Project Finance Model

## 🚀 Executive Summary
This repository contains a comprehensive 10-Year Project Finance and Operational Feasibility Model for a premium commercial education training center. The model evaluates the structural viability of launching an initial facility under intense regional competition and rigid scheduling constraints, financed by a 7-year amortizing debt facility. 

The analysis proves that by optimizing labor capacity and classroom yields, a single strategic location can scale to generate **$11 Million in terminal year-10 income**, establishing a scalable blueprint for multi-location expansion.

## 💼 Core Business Challenges Modeled
As an experienced operator in the high-competition international education sector, this model was architected to solve four critical operational bottlenecks:
1. **Perishable Inventory & Room Capacity**: Classrooms suffer from high "deadweight" during off-peak hours. The model matches room availability against strict family scheduling constraints.
2. **Hard Labor Constraints**: Prevents scheduling conflicts by ensuring a 1:1 teacher-to-class ratio while maintaining equitable workload distribution to minimize staff turnover.
3. **Market Burnout & Price Elasticity**: Accounts for a capped Total Addressable Market (TAM) where high student churn from academic burnout and aggressive competitor pricing limits arbitrary tuition hikes.
4. **Uncontrolled Customer Acquisition Costs (CAC)**: Evaluates the inflection point where hiring additional sales representatives shifts growth from linear expansion to capital-draining, uncontrolled overhead.

---

## 🛠️ Financial & Analytical Methodology
* **Dynamic Revenue Drivers**: Modeled multi-variable top-line growth tracking student enrollment counts, dynamic tuition pricing, and sales-force conversion velocities.
* **Debt Service Optimization**: Structured a 7-year debt facility to monitor the Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR), ensuring cash flows comfortably cover principal and interest obligations.
* **Labor & Resource Elasticity**: Built capacity matrices calculating the exact headcount scaling required to maximize net profit margin without over-hiring fixed overhead.
* **10-Year Long-Term Horizon**: Projected long-term capital allocation strategies to prove financial viability for eventual multi-unit rollout.
